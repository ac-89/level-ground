WHERE GRIT MEETS OPPORTUNITY

At Level Ground (LG), urban youth cultivate grit and resilience through Mixed Martial Arts. They learn to apply these values 'off the mat' in the pursuit of their ambitions through our Student Trainer Certification and College Access programs.

Watch the Video

WHY

On top of the obvious physical benefits, Level Ground training provides a sense of community and belonging, as participants train together and develop supportive relationships with coaches and teammates. We also teach important values such as sportsmanship, fair play, and respect for opponents, which can be applied to all areas of life.
